# Iceland Football — 4. & 5. Division Outreach List

Source: [ksi.is — Öll mót](https://www.ksi.is/oll-mot/) (2026 season, pulled 17 May 2026).
Socials hand-verified via web search; flagged where uncertain.

## Quick notes

- **No women's 4./5. deild exists.** The Icelandic women's pyramid stops at **2. deild kvenna** (Besta deild → Lengjudeild → 2. deild). I've included 2. deild kvenna as the equivalent grassroots tier for women's outreach.
- 4. deild karla is a **single 10-team group** in 2026 (not split into A/B/C/D as in some past years).
- 5. deild karla runs as two 8-team groups (A & B riðill).
- "FB only" is common — many small Icelandic clubs aren't on Instagram. Facebook is the better outreach channel for most of these.

## Outreach tips

- **Facebook Page Messenger** works for almost every club above. Many smaller Icelandic clubs check Page messages more reliably than Instagram DMs.
- **Best Instagram DM bets** (active accounts, fastest replies likely): Vængir Júpiters, Hafnir, Smári, Þorlákur, KM, Kría, Stokkseyri, BF 108, Fjölnir, Afturelding, Vestri, Fylkir, Augnablik, ÍR.
- **Reserve / development teams** that route through a parent club: SR → Þróttur Rvk, KÞ → Þróttur Rvk, Léttir → ÍR, KB → Leiknir Rvk. Message the parent.
- **No social presence at all**: Afríka. Route via KSÍ (ksi@ksi.is, +354 510 2900) if you really want them.
- 3 clubs appear in both a men's and women's league (Álftanes, KFR, Smári) — one message to the club covers both teams.
