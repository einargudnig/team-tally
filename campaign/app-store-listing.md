## Description
Team Tally is the simplest way to run your team's fine box.

Late to training? Missed a penalty? Forgot your kit? Log the fine in
seconds and let everyone see who owes what. No spreadsheets, no group-chat
arguments, no awkward chasing.

WHY TEAMS LOVE IT
• Set up your own fine types and amounts in minutes
• Tap to fine a player — the tally updates instantly
• See a live leaderboard of who owes the most
• Share the leaderboard as an image to the team chat
• Reminders so nobody forgets to pay up

BUILT FOR REAL TEAMS
• Works completely offline — no signal needed at the pitch
• No accounts, no sign-ups, no passwords
• Your data stays on your phone. We collect nothing.
• Free.

Made for grassroots football, but works for any squad with a fine box.
