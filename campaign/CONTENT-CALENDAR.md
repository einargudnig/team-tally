## Notes on timing

- **Day -6 is Tue 17 June — Icelandic National Day (Þjóðhátíðardagurinn).** Everyone's
  off and on their phones, but feeds are flooded with flag posts. The tease schedule
  deliberately skips it; if you want, drop a light flag-themed story ("sektir taka sér
  ekki frí 🇮🇸") but don't put a key post there.
- **Match weekends** (Day -4 Fri, Day +4 Sat) are intentional — that's when fines
  actually happen and clubs are most receptive.
- Run the **club DMs** (`team-tally-outreach-templates.md`) starting Day 0 and over the
  following week, exactly as that file's own plan lays out. The public posts are the
  backdrop; the DMs are the conversion.

## Cross-posting rules

- **IG → FB:** same copy works for both (audience overlaps, both Icelandic). Post
  natively on each rather than IG's auto-share — FB reach is better with a native post,
  and many target clubs are FB-only.
- **Don't** cross-post the Icelandic IG/FB copy to LinkedIn. LinkedIn gets the English
  founder post only.
- Put the **teamtally.is** link in the IG bio (IG kills in-caption links); use a real
  link on FB and LinkedIn.
